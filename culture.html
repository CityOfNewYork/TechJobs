Our culture is all about solving complex challenges in order to create massive impact and to get smarter about how we deliver public services.




01

Solve the greatest urban challenges.

Shape the future of New York City by using your development, data, and design skills to find innovative solutions.


02

Do what you're passionate about.

Do public health, civic engagement, or disaster response capture your imagination? You'll be able to dive right in and explore your passions.


03

Innovate in the largest urban lab.

New York City is 300 square miles of proving ground for you to put your best ideas to the test.


04

Help millions of New Yorkers.

We’re at 8.4 million New Yorkers and counting. You want to work at scale? Everything we do is at scale.


05

Set an example for the world.

Everyone looks to New York City as the gold standard. The work you do here will influence cities and governments around the world.


06

Make it here - make it anywhere.

Frank Sinatra wasn’t kidding. If you can prove yourself here, you'll launch your dream career and be a star.


07

Upgrade your skill set.

There are some skills that you can only learn working in government, such as hacking bureaucracy and creating tech and data policy.


08

Get street cred.

Do you want to be known as an innovator? It's time to get your street cred working in government. Working for the City is one of the most prestigious jobs you'll ever have.


09

Join our community.

The City is full of innovators just like you. They believe in better government, delivered digitally. Come join our community and meet the next generation of public leaders.
